Organizational Structure

Vertical — Centralized Oversight
  • Clear business principles for consistency
  • Business segments control their own markets
  • Corporate management oversees risk, compliance & finance
  • Governed by Board of Directors, CEO Jamie Dimon, & Operating Committee
Horizontal — Functional Dept.
  • Risk Management
  • Finance
  • Technology
  • Corporate Responsibility
Horizontal — Customer Dept.
  • Consumer & Community Banking
  • Corporate & Investment Banking
  • Commercial Banking
  • Asset & Wealth Management
  • Corporate Division (centralized functions)

Reduces duplication of effort and lowers costs across divisions.

Strategic Management

Corporate-Level
  • Global expansion (Asia, Middle East, Europe)
  • Supported by fortress balance sheet
  • Universal bank model across retail, investment, commercial & wealth
  • Diversification balances risk & stabilizes earnings
Industry-Level
  • Competes on quality & innovation, not price
  • Heavy investment in tech, AI & blockchain
  • Strong brand enables premium pricing
Firm-Level
  • Aggressive expansion into competitor-dominated markets
  • Sets the pace for the industry
  • Invests earlier & heavier in tech and branches

SWOT Analysis

Strength
Robust diversity and inclusion programs that enhance employee skill sets, professional knowledge, and leadership development.
Weakness
Operations are complex and costly due to diverse business sectors and large physical branch networks.
Opportunity
Emphasis on ESG initiatives can improve reputation, attract and retain investors, and support long-term sustainability.
Threat
Exposure to high credit risk and potential loan defaults that could lead to significant financial losses.

Recommendations

Strength
D&I Programs Set SMART goals and KPIs to expand diversity and inclusion programs across all global locations.
Weakness
Operational Complexity Leverage AI and digital solutions to streamline operations and reduce overhead costs.
Opportunity
ESG Initiatives Apply participative leadership to involve employees in ESG and sustainability strategies.
Threat
Credit Risk Strengthen risk management practices to minimize credit risk, loan defaults, and potential losses.
